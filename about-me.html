About Me

My name is Taha Mousavi. I'm a 39 year old designer based in Iran. During my 12 years of experience, I have worked with successful startups and brands in Iran like; Divar, TAPSI, Torob, and Porsline.

After working as a multi-disciplinary designer and designing products used by millions of Iranian people, I am eager to work with international and multicultural companies. I want to learn more about other cultures and nations and design products with and for them.
