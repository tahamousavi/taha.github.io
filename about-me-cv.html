About Me

My name is Taha Mousavi. In 10 years, I've worked as the first and sole designer in the most successful startups in Iran like Divar, TAPSI, Torob, and Porsline. In 2021, I rejoined Porsline to lead product design. 

After working as a multi-disciplinary product designer in startups and designing products for millions of Iranian people, I am eager to work with international and multicultural companies to learn more about other cultures and nations and design things with and for them.

Porsline, Head of Design (2021)

I rejoined Porsline in 2021 to lead product design. After a successful lunch in 2017 in Iran, the company decided to expand its market to the middle east countries. I am responsible to establish a design team and branching design to the market research team to improve the products for more diverse users and new business demands. 

Torob, Senior Product Designer (2019)

I joined Torob startup as a sole designer and directly worked with the Founder, CTO, and Engineers to redesign the UX and UI of Torob platforms. I also was responsible to develop a data-driven approach in product design.

Miare, Senior Product Designer (2017)

Miare was a small team of 10 when I joined them. As the only designer in the team, I was responsible for product design, including user research, designing UI of restaurant and driver applications, and doing usability tests. Miare was a fast-growing startup and we worked together to quickly improve products and services based on periodic usability tests and users feedback.

Porsline, product Designer (2017)

I joined a small team of 4 in Porsline startup. As a sole designer, I was responsible for designing MVPs, prototypes, user research, and visual design.

TAPSI, Product Designer (2016)

My role at the Tap30 startup was designing MVPs and prototypes of passengers application. At that moment, on-demand drive was a new experience in Iran, so there were a lot of challenges in passengers' and drivers' experiences. As a sole designer, I directly work with CTO, PMs, Customer Care Manager, and engineers to handle challenges and improve passengers' experience.

Divar, Product Designer (2014)

As the only designer in Divar, I was responsible for the design of all client-side products. I have worked with PMs and engineers to improve the users' experience and create consistency in the user interface on all products. Cafe Bazaar, Product Designer (2013)

I was the first designer at Cafe Bazaar. I was responsible for UX and UI design, visual designer, and Front-End Development.
